About Us

Our Story

At Orisun, flowers are more than blooms—they embody love, culture, and tradition.
We honor Africa’s heritage with sustainable, handcrafted arrangements rooted in its rich history.

Orisun means "origin" in Yoruba, reflecting our deep bond with nature.
We partner with local growers to create artful, eco-friendly bouquets.

Our flowers tell stories of joy, unity, and resilience,
celebrating the vibrant spirit and timeless beauty of African culture.
