    Ethearal Elegance

    200 kr

    The Ethereal Elegance arrangement is a stunning blend of classic romance and modern floral artistry.
    Featuring a lush selection of blooms in soft blush, ivory, and deep plum,
    this design is perfect for upscale home decor, special occasions,
    or thoughtful gifting.

    Floral Components
  • White Peonies – These lush, full blooms symbolize prosperity and romance, serving as the arrangement’s centerpiece.
  • Blush & Champagne Roses – Timeless and elegant, they add softness and refinement.
  • Ranunculus – With their delicate, layered petals in peachy hues, they bring a touch of whimsy.
  • Dark Calla Lilies – Their deep, velvety plum shade adds contrast and a modern edge.
  • Spray Carnations & Astrantia – Adding texture and depth with their intricate, star-like blossoms.
  • Seasonal Greenery & Branch Accents – Including olive leaves, ferns, and delicate twigs to enhance movement and a natural aesthetic.
    • Vase

    Arranged in a matte gold cylindrical vase, this bouquet complements both contemporary and classic interiors.
    The organic flow and asymmetry make it appear effortlessly elegant, perfect as a statement piece for any setting.
