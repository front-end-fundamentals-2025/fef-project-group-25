    WHAT IS YOUR REFUND POLICY?

    If you're not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours, and we will either replace the flowers or issue a refund.

    CAN I SCHEDULE A FLOWER DELIVERY IN ADVANCE?

    Yes, you can pre-schedule deliveries for any future date during checkout to ensure your flowers arrive on time for special occasions.

    DO YOU OFFER INTERNATIONAL FLOWER DELIVERY?

    We currently offer international flower delivery through our network of trusted floral partners. Availability and pricing may vary based on location.

    CAN I MODIFY OR CANCEL MY ORDER AFTER PLACING IT?

    Modifications and cancellations are allowed within 12 hourds of placing your order. Please contact our support team as soon as possible to request changes.
